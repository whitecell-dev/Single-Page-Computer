How Physics Engines Work

Particle System

Particles are the basic units in many physics simulations. Each particle has properties like position, velocity, mass, and forces acting upon it.

Forces & Motion

Newton's laws of motion govern how particles move. Forces like gravity, friction, and collisions are calculated each frame to update particle positions.

Collision Detection

The engine detects when particles collide with each other or boundaries, then calculates appropriate responses based on physics principles.

Numerical Integration

Equations of motion are solved using numerical methods like Euler integration or Verlet integration to approximate continuous physics in discrete time steps.
